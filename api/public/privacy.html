Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

NarrateIQ is built by adjusters, for adjusters. We do not sell your data. We do not store your claim narratives. We do not collect information about the people you are adjusting claims for. This policy explains exactly what we do collect and why.

Who we are

NarrateIQ is a product of Mouseion Fab Works. We build tools for property insurance professionals. You can reach us at info@narrateiq.ai.

What we collect

We collect the minimum necessary to operate the service:

  • Email address — used for account creation, login, and product communications. Never sold or shared with third parties.
  • Usage signals — anonymous counts of narratives generated, claim types used, and feedback ratings (Ready / Needed Edits / Not Usable). No narrative content is stored.
  • Token usage — anonymized API usage data used to monitor service cost and performance. Not linked to identifiable information.
  • Session data — standard authentication tokens stored securely on your device using iOS SecureStore. Used to keep you logged in.

Your field notes and generated narratives are never stored on our servers. They exist only on your device during your session and are cleared when you start a new claim. You control your data.

What we do not collect

  • Insured names, addresses, or personal information
  • Claim numbers or carrier information
  • Voice recordings — transcription happens on-device and only the text is used
  • Location data
  • Photos or camera content
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Device identifiers beyond what Expo/React Native requires for push notifications

How we use what we collect

  • Email — account authentication and product updates. You can unsubscribe from non-essential communications at any time.
  • Usage signals — improving the product, understanding which claim types are most used, identifying where the AI output needs improvement.
  • Token data — managing API costs and ensuring the service remains affordable.

Third-party services

NarrateIQ uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic Claude API — processes your field notes to generate narratives. Notes are sent to Anthropic's API and subject to Anthropic's privacy policy. Notes are not used to train Anthropic's models under their API terms.
  • Supabase — stores account credentials and anonymous usage data. Subject to Supabase's privacy policy.
  • Railway — hosts the NarrateIQ backend API. Subject to Railway's privacy policy.
  • Apple Speech Recognition — used on iOS for voice transcription. Subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Data retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing info@narrateiq.ai. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Beta period

NarrateIQ is currently in beta. During this period, we may collect additional diagnostic information to identify and fix issues. This information is anonymized and used solely for product improvement. We will update this policy before any changes to data collection practices.

Children

NarrateIQ is a professional tool intended for adults working in the insurance industry. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

We will notify registered users by email of any material changes to this privacy policy before they take effect. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the current version.
